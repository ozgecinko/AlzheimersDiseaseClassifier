Alzheimer's Disease

Alzheimer's disease is the most common type of dementia. It is a progressive disease beginning with mild memory loss and possibly leading to loss of the ability to carry on a conversation and respond to the environment. Alzheimer's disease involves parts of the brain that control thought, memory, and language.

Using this website, you can find out if an MRI image is Alzheimer's disease. It is classified according to four different stages of Alzheimer's disease.

  1. Mild Demented
  2. Moderate Demented
  3. Non Demented
  4. Very Mild Demented

Upload your MRI image, then learn if you have Alzheimer's disease or not.

PS: This project cannot be used for serious medical purposes.
